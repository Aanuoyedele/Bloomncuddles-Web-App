Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe for children? expand_more

Absolutely. We prioritize safety with COPPA and GDPR compliance. There are no ads, no external links, and children access the platform via secure classroom QR codes without needing personal accounts or email addresses.

How do schools get licensed? expand_more

Schools can start with a free trial. Once ready, you can choose a plan that fits your size. Our onboarding team assists with the setup of your digital classroom and provides training materials for your teaching staff.

What devices are supported? expand_more

Bloomncuddles is a web-based platform that works on any modern tablet, interactive whiteboard, or desktop computer. We recommend tablets for student use to facilitate tactile learning and mobility.
